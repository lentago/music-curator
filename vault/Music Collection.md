# Music Collection

A taste map of the collection. Open the **graph view** (Ctrl/Cmd-G): every artist sits in one branch of a two-tier **category** tree, each top-level category is its own color, combo acts link straight to the members they share, and **session ties** wire artists together through shared personnel. Nothing is pre-weighted — the densest nodes are whatever the connectivity makes them.

- **554** artists
- **13** top-level categories · **17** subcategories
- **40** collaboration edges · **396** session-tie edges (shared personnel)
