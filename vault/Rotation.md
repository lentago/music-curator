# Rotation

The **listening lens** over the collection. The shelf is a *historical* taste artifact; streaming shows what is *currently* in play, and the two do not fully overlap. Every artist carries a `rotation` class derived from the Spotify Extended Streaming History export (as of **2026-07-11**):

- **current** — 10+ plays in the trailing 18 months
- **dormant** — streamed at some point, but under that bar lately
- **historical** — in the collection, effectively absent from the stream

Open the **graph view** with the `rotation` preset to see these colors laid over the taste map — where a category cluster is all slate blue, the shelf has outlived the listening.
