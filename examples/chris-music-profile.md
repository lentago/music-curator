## Discard heuristics specific to Chris

What CAN be safely treated as discard for Chris's collection (learned across 13 rounds):

- Slick Latin pop is OUT (Pausini, Sin Bandera, Montaner, Juanes, Chichi Peralta, Alejandro Fernández, Alejandro Sanz). Folkloric / nueva canción / Vallenato / bachata / electronic-tango stays.
- Smooth jazz is OUT (Boney James, Chris Botti, FOUR PLAY, The Rippingtons, Keiko Matsui, The Braxton Brothers).
- Operatic crossover pop is OUT (Andrea Bocelli, Il Divo).
- Hallmark light classical is OUT (Emile Pandolfi, Eric Daub, Royal Phil x Phil Collins, Esterhazy Sinfonia, London Film Score Orch).
- Hype-cycle 2008-2014 indie singletons mostly OUT (Foster The People, Animal Collective MPP, Phoenix WAP, The Lumineers, CYHSY, Lady Sovereign, Santigold, The Bravery, Royal Blood, Joss Stone, Foster The People, Divine Fits, Django Django, Flobots, Eisley).
- Universal "everyone had this" sole-album downloads OUT (Adele 21, Dido No Angel, Macy Gray, Bob Marley *Legend*, Sheryl Crow *TNMC*, Jack Johnson *Brushfire*).
- TV-theme novelties / comedian albums in music collection / ringtone files / podcast folders OUT.
- Generic budget comps OUT (20 Gospel Greats, 20 Sweet Soul Classics, Pure Funk, Motown Chartbusters, Drew's Bachelorette Party, Cool Runnings, etc.).
- Phase-branded chillout comp series OUT (The Chillout Session 2002, Vol. 2, Best of Chillout Past and Present, Afterhours 3, Essential Lounge Vol. 2, Essential Selection Vol. 1, Afro-Latin Party).

What CANNOT be treated as discard despite looking like discard:
- Sole-entry "Greatest Hits" of foundational figures (Aretha, JB, Cole, Guaraldi, Neil Young — all kept).
- Industrial / EBM entries (Skinny Puppy, White Zombie were initially-suspect, all confirmed authentic).
- Early-90s alt-funk-rock microscene (Mindfunk, Fungo Mungo, Psychefunkapus all confirmed authentic — Chris's college era).
- "From a friend's bachelor party" lounge-comp territory (Ultra-Lounge series — kept).
- Niche-folk-from-foreign-country (Italian peasant-folk, Venezuelan folk vocal groups — actually mostly killed for Chris specifically, but methodology says verify rather than assume).
